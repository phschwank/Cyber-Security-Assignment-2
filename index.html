Data Protection Self Assessment

Good information handling makes good business sense.

This self-assessment tool was developed specifically for small organisations. It is particularly useful for small to medium-sized organisations in the private, public and third sectors.

Good information management makes good business sense for a company. It will improve the reputation of an organisation, increase customer and employee confidence and save time and money by ensuring that personal information is accurate, relevant and secure.

With our checklists, you can check your compliance with data protection laws and find out what steps you need to take to ensure you keep data compliant with the law. Upon completion of each checklist, a short feedback is available for you to suggest practical actions you can take to improve your data protection compliance.
