Make sure your employees are aware how their personal data is applied and what their rights are based on GDPR.

Specification — What is 'Personal Data'?

Personal data is information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. Personal data only includes information relating to natural persons who:

  • can be identified or who are identifiable, directly from the information in question; or
  • who can be indirectly identified from that information in combination with other information.
  • list of identifiers, including:
    • name;
    • identification number;
    • location data; and
    • an online identifier.
